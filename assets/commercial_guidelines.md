# Commercial Guidelines — Fee Structures, Rates & Payment Terms

> These guidelines define acceptable commercial terms for all LiverX procurement
> engagements. Any vendor proposal outside these parameters requires sign-off from
> the CFO and CPO before evaluation can proceed.

---

## 1. Accepted Fee Structures

LiverX accepts the following contract types. Vendors must specify which structure
applies and provide full justification in their commercial proposal:

| Structure | Applicability | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| **Fixed-Price** | Preferred for well-defined scope | Full deliverable breakdown required; change order process mandatory |
| **Time & Materials (T&M)** | Accepted for R&D, advisory, or ambiguous scope | Must include day rates, monthly burn cap, and not-to-exceed (NTE) ceiling |
| **Milestone-Based** | Accepted for phased projects | Payment triggers must be objective and verifiable (not % complete) |
| **Retainer** | Accepted for ongoing advisory only | Monthly cap required; unused hours do not roll over |

1.1 Hybrid structures (e.g., fixed-fee Phase 1 + T&M Phase 2) are acceptable if
    each phase is documented separately with its own budget ceiling.

1.2 Success-fee or gain-share arrangements require CFO pre-approval and are evaluated
    on a case-by-case basis.

---

## 2. Rate Card — Seniority Benchmarks

Vendor day rates and hourly rates must fall within these LiverX-approved market ranges.
Proposals exceeding the ceiling by more than **20%** will be flagged for financial review.

| Seniority Level | Hourly Rate (USD) | Daily Rate (USD, 8hr day) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Junior (0–3 yrs exp) | $40 – $80 | $320 – $640 | Requires senior supervision |
| Mid-Level (3–7 yrs exp) | $80 – $150 | $640 – $1,200 | Independent delivery expected |
| Senior (7–12 yrs exp) | $150 – $250 | $1,200 – $2,000 | Must lead workstream |
| Principal / Architect (12+ yrs) | $250 – $400 | $2,000 – $3,200 | Strategic or governance role |
| Partner / Director (client-facing) | $400 – $600 | $3,200 – $4,800 | Requires pre-approval for >5% of budget |

2.1 Rates must be quoted **exclusive of VAT** and applicable withholding taxes.

2.2 Vendors with Jordan-based delivery centres may quote in **JOD** at equivalent
    USD rates. All evaluation comparisons will use the contract-signing exchange rate.

---

## 3. Payment Terms

3.1 Standard payment terms are **Net-30** from receipt of a valid, approved invoice.

3.2 Maximum payment terms that LiverX will accept are **Net-60**. Proposals requiring
    faster payment (e.g., Net-15) must be justified and approved by Finance.

3.3 Invoices must:
   - Reference the LiverX **Purchase Order (PO) number**
   - Itemise all deliverables against the agreed milestone/SoW
   - Be submitted in **PDF format** to ap@liverx.com
   - Be denominated in the currency agreed at contract signing

3.4 Incomplete or non-compliant invoices will be returned; the payment clock resets
    upon receipt of a corrected invoice.

3.5 LiverX reserves the right to **withhold payment** for deliverables not formally
    accepted by the designated project owner.

---

## 4. Agreement Redline Policy

4.1 Vendors may propose **redlines to LiverX's standard contract** on a negotiated basis.

4.2 Acceptable redlines: payment timing, delivery SLAs, liability caps, notice periods.

4.3 Non-negotiable clauses (no redlines accepted):
   - Governing law (Jordan)
   - IP ownership (work-for-hire)
   - Data protection obligations
   - Anti-bribery and anti-corruption representations

4.4 Vendors submitting their own contract templates in lieu of LiverX's standard
    agreement will not be evaluated — LiverX's template governs.

---

## 5. Penalty & SLA Clauses

5.1 **Service Uptime SLA:** For technology or managed service contracts, vendors must
    guarantee **99.5% monthly uptime**. Downtime is calculated on a rolling 30-day basis.

5.2 **Incident Response SLA:** Critical incidents must receive an initial response
    within **4 business hours** and a resolution plan within **24 hours**.

5.3 **Late Delivery Penalty:** **1.5% of the milestone value per week** of delay, capped
    at **10% of total contract value**. Penalties are deducted from the next invoice.

5.4 **Quality Revision Penalty:** Each revision cycle beyond the agreed number (default: 2)
    incurs a **$500 flat-rate credit** per additional cycle, deducted from final payment.

5.5 Vendors may invoke **Force Majeure** for events beyond reasonable control, but must
    notify LiverX within **48 hours** of the triggering event and propose a recovery plan.

---

## 6. Currency Requirements

6.1 All contracts are denominated in **USD** by default.

6.2 **JOD (Jordanian Dinar)** is accepted for Jordan-domiciled vendors upon request.

6.3 No other currencies are accepted without written approval from the LiverX CFO.

6.4 If a contract spans more than 12 months, a **foreign currency adjustment clause**
    must be included: LiverX will absorb fluctuations up to ±5%; beyond that threshold,
    parties negotiate a revised rate at the annual review point.

---

## 7. Travel & Expense Policy

7.1 All T&E must be pre-approved and align with LiverX's expense policy:
   - **Flights:** Economy class for all trips; business class only for flights >8 hours
   - **Hotels:** Maximum USD 200/night (Amman), USD 300/night (other major cities)
   - **Meal per diem:** USD 60/day all-inclusive
   - **Receipts** required for all expenses >USD 25; submitted within 30 days of incurring
