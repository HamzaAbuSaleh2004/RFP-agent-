# Legal Guidelines — Vendor & Procurement Requirements 

> These guidelines define the minimum legal standards every vendor must meet.
> Procurement and Legal teams should update this document annually or after any
> regulatory change. Guidelines here are read by the RFP Agent at draft time.

---

## 1. Conflict of Interest

1.1 Every vendor must submit a signed **Conflict of Interest Declaration** as part
    of their bid package. No bid will be evaluated without this document.

1.2 Vendors must disclose any current, pending, or historical business relationship
    with LiverX employees, board members, or their immediate family members.

1.3 Declarations must be signed by an authorised officer of the vendor (VP level or
    above) and notarised for contracts exceeding USD 500,000.

---

## 2. Minimum Insurance Requirements

Vendors must maintain the following insurance policies throughout the engagement and
provide current certificates of insurance before contract execution:

| Coverage Type | Minimum Limit |
|---|---|
| General Liability (Commercial) | USD 2,000,000 per occurrence / USD 4,000,000 aggregate |
| Professional Indemnity (E&O) | USD 5,000,000 per claim |
| Cyber Liability | USD 1,000,000 per occurrence |
| Workers' Compensation | As required by applicable law (minimum statutory) |

2.1 LiverX must be named as an **Additional Insured** on all General Liability and
    Cyber Liability policies.

2.2 Vendor must provide **30 days written notice** of cancellation or material change
    to any required policy.

2.3 Insurance must be placed with carriers rated **A- or better** by AM Best.

---

## 3. Governing Law & Jurisdiction

3.1 All contracts arising from LiverX RFPs shall be governed by and construed in
    accordance with the **laws of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan**.

3.2 Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the competent
    courts of **Amman, Jordan**, unless resolved through arbitration per Section 4.

---

## 4. Dispute Resolution

4.1 **Step 1 — Negotiation:** Parties must attempt in good faith to resolve disputes
    within **30 calendar days** of written notice.

4.2 **Step 2 — Mediation:** If unresolved, disputes proceed to formal mediation
    administered by the **Amman Chamber of Commerce Mediation Centre**.

4.3 **Step 3 — Arbitration:** If mediation fails within 60 days, disputes are
    referred to binding arbitration under the **Amman Arbitration Centre (AAC) Rules**.
    The arbitration shall be conducted in Arabic and English, with a panel of
    three arbitrators. The seat of arbitration is Amman, Jordan.

---

## 5. NDA & Confidentiality

5.1 Vendors must sign LiverX's standard **Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)** before
    receiving any technical specifications, budget information, or evaluation criteria.

5.2 The NDA period is **5 years** from the date of signing.

5.3 Vendors may not disclose their participation in any LiverX procurement process
    without prior written consent from the LiverX Chief Procurement Officer.

5.4 All work product, deliverables, and data accessed during the engagement are
    **work-for-hire** and become the sole property of LiverX upon creation.

---

## 6. Data Protection & Privacy

6.1 Vendors handling personal data on behalf of LiverX must comply with the
    **Jordan Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)** and its implementing regulations.

6.2 A **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)** must be executed before any personal data
    is transferred to the vendor or its sub-processors.

6.3 In the event of a data breach, the vendor must notify LiverX within **72 hours**
    of becoming aware of the incident, with a full incident report within 5 business days.

6.4 Vendor must maintain data residency within the **Middle East & Africa (MEA) region**
    unless explicitly approved in writing by LiverX's Data Protection Officer.

---

## 7. Subcontractor Disclosure

7.1 Vendors must disclose **all subcontractors** who will perform any portion of the
    contracted work at the time of bid submission.

7.2 No new subcontractors may be engaged after contract award without written approval
    from LiverX Procurement.

7.3 The prime vendor remains fully liable for the acts and omissions of all
    subcontractors as if performed by the prime vendor directly.

7.4 Subcontractors must meet the same insurance and compliance standards as the prime
    vendor.

---

## 8. Anti-Bribery & Anti-Corruption

8.1 Vendors must comply with the **Jordanian Anti-Corruption Commission Law** (Law No. 62
    of 2006 as amended) and, where applicable, the **U.S. Foreign Corrupt Practices Act
    (FCPA)** and the **UK Bribery Act 2010**.

8.2 Any vendor found to have offered, paid, or facilitated any bribe or improper
    inducement will be immediately disqualified and reported to relevant authorities.
